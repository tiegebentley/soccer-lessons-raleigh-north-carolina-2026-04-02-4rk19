Our Clinic Types

Specialized training sessions for every position and skill level

Striker Clinic

Master finishing, positioning, and movement in the attacking third.

  • Clinical finishing techniques
  • Movement off the ball
  • 1v1 vs. defenders
  • Headers and volleys

Goalkeeper Clinic

Comprehensive training for shot-stopping, distribution, and command.

  • Shot-stopping technique
  • Distribution & footwork
  • Cross handling
  • 1v1 situations

Midfielder Clinic

Develop vision, passing, and control in the center of the pitch.

  • Passing under pressure
  • Field awareness & vision
  • Transition play
  • Through balls & assists

Defender Clinic

Learn defensive positioning, tackling, and building from the back.

  • Defensive positioning
  • Tackle timing & technique
  • Aerial duels
  • Playing out from the back

Speed & Agility

Improve acceleration, change of direction, and overall athleticism.

  • Sprint mechanics
  • Quick footwork drills
  • Change of direction
  • Soccer-specific conditioning

Advanced Skills

Master advanced moves, tricks, and game-changing techniques.

  • Advanced ball mastery
  • 1v1 moves & feints
  • Creativity training
  • Game application
